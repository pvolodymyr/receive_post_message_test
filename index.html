Compare and buy breakdown protection from top providers

Chaiz is an online marketplace designed to help consumers compare and buy extended vehicle warranties. It aggregates offerings from multiple third-party auto warranty providers, allowing users to research, compare, and purchase vehicle protection plans entirely online.

Chaiz offers three main types of coverage plans:

  • Powertrain Coverage:

    Covers essential components like the engine, transmission, and drive axles.

  • Powertrain Plus Coverage:

    Includes everything in the Powertrain plan plus additional components such as brakes, steering, and air conditioning.

  • Most Comprehensive (Bumper-to-Bumper) Coverage:

    Covers almost all vehicle components, listing only the exclusions.

Users can enter their car's details (make, model, year, license plate, and odometer reading) to view available plans, compare them based on coverage and price, and select the most suitable one.
